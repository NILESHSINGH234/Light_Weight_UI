Badge

Badges are used to display status information, cart item count, notification count etc.

Badge on Avatars

The badge on avatar is available in 4 different statuses - online, offline, Idle & DND.

Badge Sizes

The badge on avatar is available in 5 different sizes - avatar-xl | avatar-lg | avatar-md | avatar-sm | avatar-xs

Badge on Icons

The badge on icon has 3 different icons - shopping bag, notification & message.
